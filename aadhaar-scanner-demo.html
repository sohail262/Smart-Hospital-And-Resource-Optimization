How to Use the Aadhaar Scanner

1

Open Patient Registration

Navigate to the Patients page and click "Add Patient"

2

Click "Scan Aadhaar"

In the Add Patient modal, click the green "Scan Aadhaar" button

3

Choose Scanning Method

Either use your camera to capture the Aadhaar card or upload an existing image

4

Review Extracted Data

Check the automatically extracted information and validation results

5

Fill Patient Form

Click "Fill Patient Form" to auto-populate the registration form

OCR Technology

Uses advanced Optical Character Recognition (Tesseract.js) to extract text from Aadhaar cards with high accuracy.

Data Validation

Validates Aadhaar numbers using the Verhoeff algorithm and checks data formats for accuracy.

Multiple Input Methods

Supports both live camera capture and image upload for maximum flexibility.

Auto-Fill Forms

Automatically populates patient registration forms with extracted data to save time.

Latest Accuracy Improvements

Image Processing

Text Extraction

  • • Multiple pattern matching algorithms

  • • Context-aware field extraction
  • • Manual correction interface

Tips for Maximum Accuracy

Camera Capture

  • Use bright, even lighting
  • Keep the card flat and straight
  • Fill the entire frame with the card
  • Hold camera steady to avoid blur

Image Upload

  • Use high resolution (min 800x600px)
  • Ensure text is clearly readable
  • Avoid shadows and reflections
  • Use the edit feature to correct errors
